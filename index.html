A Breif History of the Spinning Taco

The Creation

The Taco was found in a South American jungle, even the best dating methods can't tell how old this is.

Is it Edible?

No, the first and only person who tried to taste it was turned into an ever-spinning tortilla.

Modern Day

As of the modern day the Spinning Taco is the reason we know what a taco is and how to make them.
The spinning motion of The Taco is seemingly perpetual, but it's not.

Future and Conclusion

In the future it is beleived the Spinning Taco will stop spinning, it will cease it's motion when the sun finally dies. The taco will persist, even if all life on Earth ceases to do so.
